The Boardroom

With over 15 years in financial management and risk assessment, my career is built on calculated moves, strict analytics, and structured long-term objectives.

Managing banking portfolios requires high-focus stamina, systematic planning, and the resilience to weather volatility. The discipline of the financial risk sector maps directly onto endurance training—every run, like every investment, requires patience, compounding execution, and clear strategic goals.

The Asphalt

What started as hiking and casual cycling transformed into a dedicated ultra-marathon and road running passion. Having successfully conquered the grueling Kathmandu Half Marathon, my compass is now firmly pointing toward completing my first full marathon.

Running is my clean slate. It teaches that pain is temporary, preparation is paramount, and consistency is the only vehicle that works. This athletic journey is sustained and powered by my biggest support team: my wonderful wife, who manages the dynamic daily chaos, alongside my son and daughter who cheer at every finish line.
